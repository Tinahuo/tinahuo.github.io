About Me

Hello there!

I am an amateur photographer from Shanghai, aspiring to record remarkable moments that life has to offer via a camera lens.

Currently in New York City, attending NYU with a major in Integrated Digital Media (IDM).

I shoot a variety of photos with a focus on cityscapes and portraits. All of my photos are shot with Leica model Q with a fixed 28mm lens.

If there are any suggestions or comments please feel free to find my information on the contact page!
